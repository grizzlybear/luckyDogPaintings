HAND PAINTED DOG PORTRAITS – A GREAT IDEA!

Why do you need a portrait of your dog(s)? Plain and simple: because they love you and vice versa. We weather balls of dog fur, sloppy kisses, chewed up shoes and “poop pick-up” duty as small trade offs for their unconditional love and companionship. They innately know when we need them, and will fiercely defend us from harm. A painted portrait will immortalize your pup and their “gaze of adoration”.

Thinking of gifting a portrait? BEST GIFT EVER! I can work with you for a surprise or with the recipient if you prefer a gift certificate. Your message can be added to a “process” card with the finished painting detailing all of the stages from drawing to completion.
